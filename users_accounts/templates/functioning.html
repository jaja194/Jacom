How It Works

Welcome to Jacom, where skilled workers and clients come together to
collaborate and create amazing projects. Here's how our platform works in just a few
simple steps:

Sign Up and Create Your Profile

For Skilled Workers:

Create an Account: Sign up for free and create a detailed profile showcasing your skills,
experience, certifications, and portfolio.

Highlight Your Expertise: Showcase your talents and describe the services you offer. The
more detailed your profile, the better chances of attracting clients.

For Clients:

Register Your Account: Sign up to post your projects and find the perfect skilled worker for
your needs.

Project Description: Provide a clear description of your project, including requirements,
budget, and timeline.

Browse Profiles and Projects

For Skilled Workers:

Explore Projects: Browse through client projects posted on our platform. Find projects that
match your skills and interests.

Initiate Contact: Reach out to clients by sending personalized messages expressing your
interest and suitability for their project.

For Clients:

Search Skilled Workers: Use our search filters to find skilled workers based on skills,
location, ratings, and more.

Review Profiles: View detailed profiles of skilled workers. Check their portfolios, ratings,
and reviews from previous clients.

Collaborate and Communicate

Open Communication: Once connected, use our messaging system to discuss project
details, clarify requirements, and negotiate terms.

Set Expectations: Clearly define project milestones, deadlines, and payment terms to avoid
any misunderstandings during the collaboration.

Get the Job Done

For Skilled Workers:

Deliver High-Quality Work: Complete the project to the best of your abilities. Provide
regular updates to the client and seek feedback.

For Clients:

Review Work: Review the work submitted by the skilled worker. Provide feedback and
suggest revisions if necessary.

Release Payment: Once satisfied with the work, release the payment to the skilled worker
through our secure payment system.

Leave Feedback and Ratings

Rate Your Experience: After completing a project, both clients and skilled workers can
leave feedback and ratings for each other. This helps build trust within our community.

Build Long-Term Relationships

Repeat Business: For skilled workers, deliver exceptional work to encourage clients to hire
you for future projects.

Find Reliable Talent: For clients, build relationships with skilled workers you trust
for ongoing collaborations.

Ready to get started? Join JACOM today and embark on a journey of creativity,
collaboration, and success!
