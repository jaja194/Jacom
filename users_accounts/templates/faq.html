How can we help you?

General Questions

Q1: What is JACOM

A: JACOM is an online marketplace that connects skilled workers with clients
looking for their expertise. Whether you're a skilled artisan, technician, designer, or
professional in any field, our platform helps you find clients or projects that match your skills.

Q2: How does JACOM work?

A: Clients can post projects and browse profiles of skilled workers. Skilled workers can
create profiles, showcase their expertise, and connect with clients. The platform facilitates
communication, collaboration, and secure transactions between clients and skilled workers.

For Clients

Q3: How do I find a skilled worker for my project?

A: You can search for skilled workers using our search filters based on skills, location,
ratings, and more. Browse through profiles, review portfolios, and message skilled workers
directly to discuss your project requirements.

Q4: Is there a cost to post a project or hire a skilled worker?

A: Posting a project is free. Fees apply only when you hire a skilled worker. The specific fees
and payment details are outlined during the hiring process.

For Skilled Workers

Q5: How do I create a profile on JACOM?

A: To create a profile, sign up for an account and complete your profile with details about
your skills, experience, and portfolio. Make sure to showcase your expertise and
certifications to attract potential clients.

Q6: Can I showcase my previous work on my profile?

A: Yes, absolutely! You can showcase your previous work, projects, certifications, and
testimonials on your profile to demonstrate your skills and expertise to potential clients.

Payments and Security

Q7: How are payments processed on JACOM?

A: Payments between clients and skilled workers are processed securely through our
platform. We offer multiple payment options to ensure a convenient and safe transaction
experience.

Q8: Is my personal information secure?

A: Yes, we take data security seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and stored
securely. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

Getting Help

Q9: How do I contact customer support?

A: If you need assistance or have any questions, you can contact our customer support team
via email at Support Email, phone at Support Phone Number, or through our Contact Us page.

Q10: What if I have a dispute with a client/skilled worker?

A: We have a dispute resolution process in place. If you encounter any issues, please contact
our support team, and we will assist you in resolving the dispute.
